Privacy Policy

Here at Froggy Studios, protecting our customers' private information and identity is something we value greatly. We are in the business of providing digital entertainment, all data and information that we collect is only used to enhance your entertainment experience. The use of our website and apps is subject to this policy. Our privacy policy may change without notice; however, our overall privacy philosophy remains constant.

Our Commitment

We are committed to the ethical handling of your personal data.

We will never transmit any personal information through cyber space in an unencrypted or unsecured way.

We will never distribute or sell any of your personal information to 3rd parties.

Collected Information

Some of our products offer a user account feature, which requires an e-mail address and password. We may send you an account verification e-mail. Additionally, we may send you a reset password e-mail.

Some of our products allow you to log in using your Facebook™ account. Depending on the permissions you grant, we may collect your e-mail address or a unique id number associated with your Facebook™ account. We only use this information from Facebook™ to verify your account.

In addition to user accounts, Froggy Studios may collect non-personal usage data for analytical purposes. Analytical data is collected towards the aim of improving our products.

Some of our apps use advertising services provided by Google™ or Amazon™. These services may collect analytical data and device identifiers, which are used to serve you relevant advertisements. Your use of certain Froggy Studios products is also subject to the privacy policies of Google Analytics™ and Amazon Mobile Ad Network™. Froggy Studios is not responsible for the privacy policies of Google™ or Amazon™. We are committed to selecting 3rd party vendors which do not have abusive privacy policies.

You can opt out from some of Google's data collection using this website:
https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout/

Froggy Studios does not directly use cookies, web beacons, or other similar technologies, however, our 3rd party vendors may use these technologies to serve you relevant advertisements. Cookies are small amounts of data that are stored in separate files within your device's internet browser. Cookies are accessed and recorded by the web-sites you visit, and by the companies that show advertisements on these web sites, so that they can recognize your browser. Web beacons are small strings of code that deliver a small graphic image. Web beacons can recognize certain types of information on your computer such as cookies, the time and date a page is viewed, and a description of the page where the web beacon is placed.

Our apps or web sites may provide you with links to affiliated content. Froggy Studios is not responsible for the privacy policies of content that we link to.

Data Storage & Transmission

Froggy Studios protects all of your information both online and off-line. The transmission of data, including any account information, is protected using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology. Private data such as your e-mail address and password are protected with Advanced Encryption Standard (AES) technology.

Some of our products have features which allow users to share creative works with other users. We do not associate any of your personal information with online content that you share. Your online id is represented with a user alias, Froggy Studios will never associate your alias with your real identity. Your community alias may be represented as moniker that you select or as a unique identifying number.
